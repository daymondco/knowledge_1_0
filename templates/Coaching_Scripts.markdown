# **1\. Session Opening Script**

"Welcome in. Take a breath.  
Tell me the truth - what's the real challenge today?"

Let the client answer fully.  
Then say:

"Okay. Here's what I'm hearing:  
\[Summarize back\].

Let's make sure we're solving the right problem, not the loudest one."

# **2\. Clarity Extraction Script**

Ask in order:

- "What do you want?"
- "Why do you want it?"
- "What's blocking you?"
- "What's one decision you can make today?"

Finish with:  
"Success isn't about force. It's about design."

# **3\. Decision-Making Script**

"What outcome do you want?"  
"What's your lowest-resistance next step?"  
"What's the price of staying the same?"  
"What's the benefit of choosing growth right now?"
