# **BlaqBrand (BlaqBrand.com)**

## **1\. Description of the Agency**

BlaqBrand is a technical-first digital infrastructure agency built to empower modern entrepreneurs with secure, fast, and professionally engineered online systems. Founded by Coach Daymond, BlaqBrand specializes in delivering enterprise-grade hosting, data storage, security solutions, and website builds that combine engineering precision with polished creative execution.

BlaqBrand intentionally centers Black entrepreneurs, Black creatives, queer entrepreneurs, and queer creatives, providing them with accessible, trustworthy, and future-ready digital foundations. The agency exists to eliminate technical barriers and give underestimated visionaries the infrastructure needed to grow, scale, and protect their digital assets.

## **2\. Services**

### **Web Hosting**

- Fast, secure, scalable hosting optimized for small and mid-sized brands.  

- Managed hosting options, uptime monitoring, and SSL included.  

### **Data Storage**

- Encrypted cloud-based storage for business assets, documents, media, and backups.  

- Redundant architecture to prevent data loss.  

### **Data Security**

- Cybersecurity monitoring, threat detection, and daily backups.  

- Security hardening, data integrity protection, and compliance-minded safeguards.  

### **Website Builds**

- Professional, responsive websites engineered for speed, functionality, and brand alignment.  

- Includes structural design, content setup, and scalable architecture.

- Map technical pathways, timelines, and deliverables.  

3\. Setup & Configuration

- Deploy servers, security layers, cloud storage, and monitoring tools.  

- Configure environments based on the client's scale and growth plan.  

4\. Website Build (if applicable)

- Create the site layout, brand integration, and experience flow.

5\. Testing & Quality Assurance

- Conduct performance testing, vulnerability scanning, and functional checks.  

- Resolve technical issues before launch.  

6\. Deployment / Launch

- Move the system live and ensure stability.  

- Provide onboarding for site management (if requested).  

7\. Ongoing Support & Monitoring

- Continuous updates, backups, threat detection, and maintenance.  

- Optional monthly or quarterly digital health reports.
